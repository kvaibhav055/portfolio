About Me

Welcome to My Cybersecurity & Development Portfolio!

Hi, I’m Vaibhav Sharma, a cybersecurity enthusiast and full-stack developer with a strong foundation in C++ and web security. My passion lies in ethical hacking, penetration testing, and secure web development, ensuring that digital platforms are resilient against modern cyber threats.

I am currently pursuing my B.Tech in Information Technology at Manipal University Jaipur (2022-2026). My technical journey is fueled by an insatiable curiosity to analyze, defend, and fortify applications against vulnerabilities. I specialize in network security, encryption, and secure coding practices, constantly exploring emerging cybersecurity trends and frameworks.

Beyond development, I actively engage in CTF (Capture The Flag) competitions, bug bounty programs, and cybersecurity research. I contribute to open-source security projects on GitHub and refine my problem-solving skills on GeeksForGeeks and other competitive programming platforms.

My projects focus on secure application development, including penetration testing tools, encrypted communication platforms, and hardened web applications that align with industry security standards.

I invite you to explore my work, and if you're passionate about cybersecurity or interested in collaboration, feel free to connect!
